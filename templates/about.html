FAQ

MyTopHHA.com is a website where you can make a list of your top Hip Hop Artists. First, you have to set your criteria based off of a list of categories on the "My Criteria" page. Next, you go to the "Rate Artists" page and select the hip hop artists you would like to rate. You will then rate the artists on a scale from 1 to 10 for each category. Your criteria settings combined with your artist ratings will give the artists a total score, and rank them in order on the "My Top Artists" page. For a full decription please visit the "About" tab.
Without registering, your settings and ratings will not be saved between sessions. This means if you ever clear your cookies or switch devices, your data will be lost. Registering to MyTopHHA.com will save your information across all sessions and devices.
Verifying your email allows you to reset your password whenever you forget it. Otherwise, you will not be able to reset your password until your email is verified.
If you did not receive an email confirmation link, first double check to make sure that your email is entered correctly on the website. If your email is correct, then please check the Spam folder in your email inbox for the confirmation link. If you still can't find the confirmation email, please contact us through the contact form on the "Contact" tab of the about page
If you are having any trouble with your account, please let us know through the contact form.
Let us know! Any feedback is welcome. Contact us through the contact form.
